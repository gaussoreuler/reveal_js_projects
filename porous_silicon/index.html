    Goals of this Labclass

    Education in safe handling of Hydrofluoric (HF) Acid : HF is an extremely corrosive inorganic acid that demands the utmost care during handling. It can easily penetrate the skin, decalcify bones, and cause tissue necrosis, potentially leading to amputation or even death. hello this is a test

    Fabrication of a dielectric mirror: Dielectric mirrors are typically fabricated using traditional deposition methods such as evaporation and sputtering using a combination of materials such as SiO2 and TiO2. However, the refractive index of silicon can be precisely controlled by varying its porosity, making it possible to create a multilayer stack of porous silicon. This stack, with alternating layers of different porosities, can function as an effective dielectric mirror by exploiting constructive interference of light. hello this is another test
